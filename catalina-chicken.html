Catalina Chicken

Serving Size: 6

Recipe by: Annie Barrera

Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Lemon Pepper seasoning
  • Catalina dressing.
Instructions:
  1. Pat dry chicken.
  2. Season chicken with Lemon Pepper seasoning.
  3. In a hot skillet, brown chicken, then drain fat from pan.
  4. After chicken is near fully cooked, turn down heat and add the Catalina dressing.
  5. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes or until done. Chicken should be tender.

üìù Notes:

Cut chicken into equal parts so it all finishes cooking at the same time. Darker meat can cook longer without drying out.
