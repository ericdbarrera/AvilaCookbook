Tasty Tea

Serving size: 4

Recipe by: Terry A. Weaver

Ingredients:
  • 1 Tea bag Luzianne
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • Lemon Slices
Instructions:
  1. In a medium sauce pan fill with water and add tea bag, then turn heat up to medium-high
  2. Before it comes to a boil remove from heat.
  3. In a large pitcher (4qts) fill halfway with water and add the sugar.
  4. Add hot tea and stir until sugar is completely dissolved.
  5. Let cool 1 hr before serving. Garnish with lemon.
