Mr. B's Lemonade

Serving size: 10-12

Recipe by: Eric Barrera

Ingredients:
  • 6 Lemons
  • 1 1⁄4 cups Sugar
  • 5 cups Cold Water
  • Ice
Instructions:
  1. WASH LEMONS
  2. Peel zest (just the yellow part) off all lemons and add to bowl.
  3. Add sugar to the peels, cover, and let sit out for 2-12 hours.
  4. Bring 5 cups of water to a boil, turn off heat and add sugar and peels. Stir till sugar is desolved.
  5. Strain liquid back into the bowl removing the peels.
  6. Let mixture cool down to room temp.
  7. Add lemon juice from peeled lemons. Move lemonade to a pitcher, cover and refrigerate
  8. Once chilled, serve over ice. Enjoy
