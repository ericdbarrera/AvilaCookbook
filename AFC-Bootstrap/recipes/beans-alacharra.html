Beans a la Charra

Serving Size: 20+

Recipe by: Evila A. Montemayor

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb Pinto beans
  • Garlic
  • 2 pkg. Bacon chopped
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Salt Pork
  • 1 bushel of cilantro, chopped
  • Pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Clean beans and wash thoroughly
  2. in a stockpot, add beans and water
  3. Add garlic cloves, salt pork and simmer for 2.5 hours covered.
  4. Once beans are almost fully cooked, in a seperate saucepan saute 1 pkg of bacon chopped.
  5. When bacon is halfway cooked add onions. Saute onions
  6. Add contents of saucepan to beans and mix well.
  7. Turn off heat and add cilantro. Do not stir the cilantro, let is set over the beans, covered at least 10 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
