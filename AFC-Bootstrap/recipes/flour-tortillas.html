Tortillas de Harina

Serving Size: 20

Recipe by: Guadalupe A. Avila

Ingredients:
  • 5 cups AP Flour
  • 1 tbs Baking Powder
  • 1 tbp Salt
  • 1 cup Shortening or 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 cup Hot Water
Instructions:
  1. In a large bowl add flour, salt, and baking powder and mix.
  2. Add shortening or butter
  3. Carefully add hot water incorporate to flour. Kneed until dough is smooth.
  4. Let dough rest in a covered bowl for 10 minutes
  5. Pinch off small balls of dough and roll out flat.
  6. Cook on a hot comal.
  7. Ready to serve.
