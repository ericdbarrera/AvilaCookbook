Carrot Pineapple Cake

Serving size: 4

Recipe by: Annie Barrera

Ingredients:
  • 11⁄2 cup A.P. Flour, sifted
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1⁄2 tsp Salt
  • 2⁄3 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Carrots, finely shredded
  • 1 cup Pecans, chopped
  • 1⁄2 cup Crushed Pineapples, with syrup
Cake Instructions:
  1. Sift together in large mixing bowl flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder cinnamon, salt. Add oil, eggs, shredded carrots, pecans, pineapple(w/ syrup) and vanilla. Mix until moistened.
  2. Beat 2 minutes at medium speed on electric mixer.
  3. Grease and lightly flour 9x9x2 in pan.
  4. Bake at 350F for 35 minutes or until done.
  5. Let cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan.
  6. After bread is cooled, frost with cream cheese.
Frosting Ingredients:
  • 1 3oz package Cream Cheese
  • 1 tsp Butter, softened
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 cups Confectioners Sugar, sifted.
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
Frosting Instructions:
  1. In a small mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, butter and vanilla
  2. Beat at low speed on electric mixer until light color.
  3. Gradually add sugar, beating until fluffy.
  4. If necessary, add milk to make spreading consistent.
  5. Stir in 1/2 cup chopped nuts, if desired.
