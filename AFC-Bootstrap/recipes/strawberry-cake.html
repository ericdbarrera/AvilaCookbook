Strawberry Cake

Serving size: 6

Recipe by: April M. Dominguez

Ingredients:
  • Strawberry cake mix
  • 20 oz Sweetened Frozen Sliced Strawberries, thawed.
Toppings:
  • 1 pkg (4 serving size) Vanilla Instant Pudding
  • Pie Filling Mix
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 2 cups Frozen Whipped Topping, thawed
  • Fresh Strawberries
Cake Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Grease and flour pan 13x9x2.
  3. Prepare, bake, and cool cake mix. Follow directions on box.
  4. Poke holes 1 inch apart on top of the cake with handle of woden spoon.
  5. Puree thawed strawberries with juice in blender.
  6. Spoon evenly over the top of the cake allowing mixture to soak in.
Toppings Instructions:
  1. Prepare pudding mix per package.
  2. Follow directions using 1 cup of milk.
  3. Fold whipped topping into pudding mixture and spread over cake.
  4. Garnish with fresh strawberries.
  5. Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.
