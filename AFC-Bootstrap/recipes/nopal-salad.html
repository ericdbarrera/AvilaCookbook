Nopalito Salad

Lent Recipe

Serving size: 4-6

Recipe by: Balboas

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Nopalitos, rinsed and cubed
  • Garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 medium tomatoes chopped.
  • 1⁄3 cup Cilantro
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 4 tbs Lime Juice or 4 tbs Red wine vinegar (optional)
  • 1⁄4tsp Oregano, dried
  • 2 Avocados cubed
  • 1 head of lettuce
Instructions:
  1. Bring a large pot of water add 1/2 onion, garlic, cloves, salt and bring to a boil.
  2. Blanch the nopales. Turn heat down to medium, and add nopales to simmering water. Remove nopales after 2 minutes. Run under cold water to stop cooking and set aside. Discard water, half onion and garlic.
  3. Chop the other half of onion, add chopped onions, nopalitos, tomatoes, cilantro, lime juice (or red wine vinegar), oregano into a large mixing bowl. Set aside for 1 hour in refrigerator.
  4. Add fresh avocado before serving.
  5. Garnish with lettuce. Lay lettuce leaves flat on platter and place nopalito salad on top.
  6. Enjoy
