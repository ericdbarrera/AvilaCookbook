Apple Pie

Serving size: 6-8

Recipe by: Dori Barrera

Crust Ingredients:
  • 2 1⁄2 cup Flour
  • 3⁄4 tsp Salt
  • 1⁄2 cup Solid Vegetable Shortening
  • 7 tbsp Butter cut into small pieces
  • 6-7 tbsp Ice Water
Crust Instructions:
  1. In a medium bowl mix flour & salt. Cut in shortening and butter with a pastry blender until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle 6 tbsp of water then stir with fork until mixture clumps together. Add remaining water if necessary.
  2. Divide dough in half. Shape each half into 1in thick disk. Wrap and refrigerate at least 1hour minimum (up to 2 days) until firm enough to roll out.
  3. Preheat oven to 425F. Prepare one 9in pie pan and foil-lined cookie sheet.
  4. With a lightly floured rolling pin, roll out half of the dough to a 12in circle. Line pie-plate with dough. Flute the edges. Refrigerate.
  5. Roll remaining dough into a 12in circle, using a knife cut dough into 1/2in strips. Lay strips on filled pie at 1in intervals. Fold back alternate strips as you weave cross strips. Seal dough with flute edge.
Filling Ingredients:
  • 3⁄4 cup Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tsp ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 7 med Granny Smith or Golden Delicious Apples,
    peeled, quartered, cored, and cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp butter cut into small pieces
  • 1 tsp Milk
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
Filling Instructions:
  1. Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon & Lemon juice in a large bowl. Add apples and toss gently to mix and coat. Spread in pie shell, trucking them to fit. Scrape any juice in bowl over apples, dot with butter.
  2. Arrange strips over pie in lattice form. Brush with milk, sprinkle with sugar.
  3. Place on lined cookie sheet and bake 15 minutes.Reduce oven temperature to 350F. Bake 50-60 minutes longer until crush is golden brown and apples are tender. Let pie cool on wire rack.
  4. Serve warm or at room temperature.
