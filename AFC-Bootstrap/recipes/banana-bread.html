Banana Nut Bread

Serving size: 8-10

Recipe by: Patricia Avila

Ingredients:
  • 1 stick of unsalted Butter - room temp
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 3⁄4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3-4 ripened Bananas
  • 1 tbs Milk
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 cup A.P. Flour
  • 3⁄4 tbs Salt
  • 1⁄2 cup Walnuts or Peacan pieces
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix softened butter, sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. In a separate bowl add eggs, smashed bananas, milk, and vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl add flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt and nuts.
  5. Add dry mix to wet mix.
  6. Grease and flour a loaf pan or use parchment paper.
  7. Pour dough into loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  8. Test doneness with a knife.

📝 Notes:

🔪 Knife Test: Test if the the loaf is cooked all the way through. Take a knife and poke the center of the loaf all the way through, remove the knife, if it is clean then the loaf done. If there is filling or dough stuck to the knife then it need more time to bake.
