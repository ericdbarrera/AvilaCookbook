Tuna Patties

Lent Recipe

Serving size: 4-6

Recipe by: Patricia Avila

Ingredients:
  • 2 cans of Tuna Fish in water- drained
  • 4 eggs beaten
  • 5 saltine crackers finely crushed
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Oil
Instructions:
  1. Drain tuna fish, put into mixing bowl along with crushed saltine crackers and salt & pepper.
  2. Add 4 eggs to mixure
  3. In a hot skillet add oil in medium-high heat slowly add a table spoon of mixture into hot oil. Careful not to splash hot oil.
  4. flip once. each side should be golden brown and crispy.
