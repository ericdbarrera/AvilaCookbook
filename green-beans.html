Really Good Green Beans

Serving size: 6-8

Recipe by: Dina Lee

Ingredients:
  • 3lbs Green Beans, trimmed of stem
  • 1/2 cup skin-on sliced almonds
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter
  • 5 tbs olive oil
  • 2 large Onions sliced thin
  • 2 tbs thyme
  • Salt & Pepper
Instructions:
  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add green beans & salt and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove green beans and run them under cold water to stop from cooking. Set aside for later. Drain water from pot.
  3. In the same pot, toast almonds over medium heat, stir every now and them until golden brown. Should take about 3-5 minutes
  4. Add butter & oil and heat till melted
  5. Add slicked onions season with salt & pepper and cook until onions carmelize, 20-25 minutes
  6. Add thyme. Cook for additional 5 minutes
  7. Add cooked green beans and toasted almonds back into pot. Stir well to combine
