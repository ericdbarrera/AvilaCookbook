Sunday Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • 1 Pot Roast or Chuck Roast
  • 4 Potatoes, sliced
  • 3 Carrots, chopped
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Water

Directions

  • Season roast with salt and pepper.
  • On the stove top, sear meat on all sides in a Dutch oven
  • Add water and vegetables and cook at 350F. for 90 minutes. Alternatively you can add meat and veggies to a crock pot on high heat
  • Cook until meat tender
