Broccoli Rice Cheese Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 box Minute Rice
  • 1 to 2 cans of Cream of Mushroom
  • 1 to 2 cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 bag Broccoli Florets (maybe two depending on the amount of persons)
  • H‑E‑B easy melt cheese or Velveta cheese

Directions

  • Preheat oven 350F
  • Boil your broccoli
  • Once your broccoli and rice is cooked in an oven casserole dish mix rice and broccoli add cream of mushroom and chicken soups. Make sure you mix all the ingredients well.
  • In a separate dish melt cheese in a microwave dish. Make sure cheese melts smoothly. Once it’s melted well add cheese to your rice, broccoli and soup.
  • Mix it all well and bake for about 45 to 50 minutes. It will be golden brown on top
  • Ready to serve.
