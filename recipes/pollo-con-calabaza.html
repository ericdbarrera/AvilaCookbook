Pollo con Calabaza

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Summer Squash, cubed
  • 1 can Corn
  • 2 tsp Beef Bullion
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Onion diced
  • Garlic
  • Cumin

Directions

  • Season chicken, and cook thoroughly
  • Add onions to pan, cook till onions are translucent
  • Add squash to pan
  • Add corn and beef bouillon and let simmer for 5 - 10 minutes
  • Ready to serve.

Recipe by: Aurora A. Avila
