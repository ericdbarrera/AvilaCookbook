Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • Potatoes cubed
  • 1 pkg Onion Soup Mix
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Carrots
  • 1 can 6oz Evaporated Milk
  • Celery
  • 8oz Velvetta Cheese

Directions

  • Fill Pot with water and boil potatoes until tender
  • Add carrots and celery till tender
  • Add remaining Ingredients. Simmer until cheese is melted.
