Pozole Rojo

Ingredients

Main pot
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 6 Garlic Cloves
  • Pork Neck, Loin, Shoulder, Feet
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • Water
  • 1 can 28oz Hominy
  • Salt & Pepper
Salsa
  • 1/2 - 2 Chili de Arbol (depending on spiciness)
  • 3 Chili Guajillo
  • 1 Chili Ancho
  • Broth from main pot
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • Ground Cumin
  • Cilantro
  • 1/2 Onions
  • 1 tsp Chicken Bouillon
  • 1 tsp Oregano
Toppings
  • Oregano
  • Sliced Radishes
  • Onion Diced
  • Lettuce shredded
  • Lime

Directions

  • Add pork, bay leaves, garlic, and onion to water and bring to a boil. Let simmer for 1.5 hours.
  • Skim off any any gunk from the top.
  • Prep chilies - Cut open and remove seeds.
  • Use broth from the main pot to secondary pot to boil chilies. simmer on low for 15 minutes or until soft.
  • ⚠️If you are using a ninja style blender. Let chilies & broth cool before blending.⚠️
  • Add chilies to blender with 3 garlic cloves, oregano, chicken bouillon, 1/4 onion and some broth. Blend mixture.
  • Use a strainer / colander to separate pulp and pour salsa into main pot.
  • Drain and rinse Hominy, add to main pot after pork is tender, Add salt. let simmer for 30 minutes.
  • if broth is too watery cook for additional time.
  • Let pozole cool for 15 minutes. Remove bay leaves.
  • Add toppings and serve
