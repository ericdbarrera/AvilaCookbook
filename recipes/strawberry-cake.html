Strawberry Cake

Ingredients

  • Strawberry cake mix
  • 20 oz Sweetened Frozen Sliced Strawberries, thawed.

Toppings

  • 1 pkg (4 serving size) Vanilla Instant Pudding
  • Pie Filling Mix
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 2 cups Frozen Whipped Topping, thawed
  • Fresh Strawberries

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350F.
  • Grease and flour pan 13x9x2.
  • Prepare, bake, and cool cake mix. Follow directions on box.
  • Poke holes 1 inch apart on top of the cake with handle of woden spoon.
  • Puree thawed strawberries with juice in blender.
  • Spoon evenly over the top of the cake allowing mixture to soak in.
Topping
  • Prepare pudding mix per package.
  • Follow directions using 1 cup of milk.
  • Fold whipped topping into pudding mixture and spread over cake.
  • Garnish with fresh strawberries.
  • Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.
