Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Dark Corn Syrup
  • 2 tbsp Butter, melted
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 1/4 cup Pecans, pieces or halves
  • 1 9inch Unbaked Pie Crust, Frozen

Directions

  • Preheat oven 350F
  • In a large bowl mix eggs, sugar, dark corn syrup, butter, until well incoporated.
  • Pour mixture into pie crust
  • Optional: decorate top of pie with pecan halves
  • Bake pie 50 - 55 minutes. Test if done with a toothpick or knife.
  • Let the pie cool on wire rack before serving

Recipe by: Patricia Avila
