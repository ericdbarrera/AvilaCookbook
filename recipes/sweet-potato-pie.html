Sweet Potato Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Sweet Potatoes, cooked and mashed
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Flour
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Pie Shells

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 375F
  • Place pie sheets in oven for 5 minutes (to cook the dough partially)
  • Mix sweet potatoes, butter, eggs, milk, flour, cinnamon, and vanilla
  • Pour mixture into pie shells and back for 30 - 40 minutes
