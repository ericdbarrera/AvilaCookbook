Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Butter, softened
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup Pecans, chopped

Directions

  • Preheat oven 375F
  • Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
  • beat butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla in a large bowl.
  • Add eggs one at a time into large bowl
  • Gradually add dry mixture to large bowl. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts
  • Form balls from cookie dough, add to greased pan 2 inches apart.
  • Bake 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  • Let cool on rack before serving.

Recipe by: Patricia Avila
