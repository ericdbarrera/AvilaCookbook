Potato Soup

Serving size: 4

Recipe by: Molly Plata Avila

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes peeled & cubed
  • 1 pkg Onion Soup Mix
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Carrots
  • 1 can 6oz Evaporated Milk
  • Celery
  • 8oz Velvetta Cheese
Instructions:
  1. Fill Pot with water and boil potatoes until tender.
  2. Add carrots and celery simmer till tender.
  3. Add remaining Ingredients. Simmer until cheese is melted.
